# 🍽 Zomato Restaurant Market Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an end-to-end exploratory data analysis of the Zomato restaurant dataset to identify customer behavior, restaurant popularity, service availability, pricing trends, and satisfaction patterns. The analysis aims to derive actionable business insights for restaurant owners and food service platforms.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze the distribution of restaurant categories
- Identify the most popular restaurant types based on customer votes
- Examine the impact of online ordering and table booking
- Study customer rating patterns
- Analyze approximate pricing trends
- Generate business recommendations from customer data

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies Used
- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

---

## 📂 Dataset Features
The dataset contains:
- Restaurant Type
- Online Order Availability
- Table Booking Availability
- Votes
- Ratings
- Approximate Cost for Two People

---

## 📊 Key Analysis Performed
- Data Cleaning and Preprocessing
- Missing Value Handling
- Duplicate Record Removal
- Univariate Analysis
- Bivariate Analysis
- Correlation Analysis
- Business Insight Extraction

---

## 🔍 Key Findings
- Dining restaurants dominate the market and receive the highest customer votes.
- Restaurants offering online ordering show higher customer engagement.
- Mid-range restaurants capture the majority customer segment.
- Premium restaurants with table booking tend to maintain better ratings.
- Customer convenience features directly influence restaurant popularity.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Increase digital ordering integration to maximize visibility.
- Maintain affordable pricing to attract larger customer base.
- Focus on customer reviews and ratings management.
- Introduce premium reservation services where applicable.
